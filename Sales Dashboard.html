Sales Dashboard

The Overview Tab shows overview shows a breakdown of each individual product line in comparison to one another. The key metrics being tracked are total sales, unique orders, and quantity ordered. The users is quickly able to identify volume trends in each product category over the desired period of time. The use of buttons always the users to focus on Total Sales or Net Sales.

The Regions tab gives a similar breakdown to Overview tab but focuses on territory sales rather than product lines. Users can quickly identify territory based trends and identify seasonal volume moments.

The final section, the Advanced tab, shows data in a raw form table. Users can filter down the data using the slicers and export the data directly to excel for use in additional analysis, distribution, or other use cases.
