Supermaket Performance Dashboard

The 'Financial Overview' tab gives a high level view of the financial performance for the company as a whole, but can be filtered down to see individual branches, product lines, and other details. MoM (DAX) calculations allow the user to quickly see the current month growth trends.

The 'Trend Tracker tab tracks the financial and volume trends of everything from branch locations to gender based sales figures.
