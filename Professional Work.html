Professional Work

In this section, I will describe some of my most recent projects in my profession career. Due to Non-Disclosure Agreements, project descriptions will not be detailed.

Financial and Operational based interactive Dashboards:

- Two Power BI Dashboards powered by a optimized, star-schema data model which is connected to the Oracle Database

- Gives users visibiltiy into their customer(s) financial and operational performance

- Advanced DAX measures allow the user to quickly see where their customer(s) are performing or declining and potential cost saving opportunities

- Based off user feedback, these Dashboards have lead to significant gains in revenue creation as users can now make data driven dicisions

Historical Lane Rating Tool:

- Tool hosted in Power BI that provides a rate based on inputed origin and destination points as well as additional criteria

- Historical data from the Transportation Management System (TMS) is analyzed based on inputed criteria and returns a suggested rate

- Additional information such as suggested carriers, common accessorial charges, and trends are returned as supplemental information

- An internal case study found that this tool significantly increased the profitability and accuracy of rates, even in current volitile market conditions.

Automations:

- Automated over 15+ hours (weekly) of tasks using scripting (python) and automation tools such as Power Automate

- These tasks included scheduled reports distributed via email or Microsoft Teams, manipulation of lists in SharePoint, and ETL processes

- Additionally, Python libraries such as pandas and matplotlib were used for data analysis
